Private Equity Warning

YouTube Channel Warning Extension

This extension warns you about YouTube channels that are owned by private equity firms.

How It Works

The extension maintains a local blacklist of private equity-owned channels. When you visit a YouTube video or channel page, it checks the channel against this list and shows a warning if there's a match.

Privacy

All matching happens locally in your browser. No browsing data is sent to any server. The blacklist is periodically updated from a public API, and updates are cached locally for performance.

Data Source

The blacklist is maintained by community contributors and updated periodically. If you believe a channel is incorrectly listed, please contact the blacklist maintainers.
